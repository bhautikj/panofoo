Simple web Virtual Reality embeds - take any images and turn them into a VR experience.

Simple

Do one job well

Not a game engine - vrEmbed is optimized to take your images and give you versatile options to display them in VR.

No apps

Use anywhere

No need for a native app - works in mobile and desktop browsers, and loads fast - the javascript is less than 120kb

Easy

Build embds fast

Simple embed codes to create a vrEmbed from any image - these can be chained together to make entire VR stories!

Accessible

Stereo blind

At least 10% of the population can't use VR headsets or see 3D - vrEmbed won't leave these users behind.

Compatible

Headset support

vrEmbed has a support for an ever-growing range of viewing modes, from red-blue glasses to Google Cardboard and beyond.

Free

Open Source

vrEmbed is open source!
